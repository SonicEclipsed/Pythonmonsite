Welcome to the World of Pokémon!

Pokémon is the best selling video game franchise in the world and it has been going strong for 25 years! With this program, you can experience the unique battles of Pokémon like never before in Python! This simulator will allow you to choose from 20+ Pokémon from the first 6 generations of the series, choose their held items, and mega evolve them! To make things more spicy, the moves of each Pokémon will be randomized! Download Pythonmon today by clicking on the "Download" button on the top of this website!

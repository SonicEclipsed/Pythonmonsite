  • Welcome to the World of Pythonmon! (Project Description)

    Pokémon is the best selling video game franchise in the world and it has been going strong for 25 years! With this program, you can experience the unique battles of Pokémon like never before in Python! This simulator will allow you to choose from 20+ Pokémon from the first 6 generations of the series to fight against an opponent! To make things more spicy, the IVs and EVs of each Pokémon will be randomized!

  • Instructions

  • How it works

    In Pythonmon, every battle will be unique! The IVs and EVs of each Pokémon will be completely random! In each battle, the player will fight against an opposing Pokémon. Every turn, the opposing Pokémon will use a random move in its moveset.

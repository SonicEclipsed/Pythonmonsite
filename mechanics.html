Pythonmon Mechanics

Randomization

In Pythonmon, every battle will be unique! Many things will be randomized at the start of each battle: The IVs, EVs, moves, ability, and held item of each Pokémon will be completely random! Of course, the moves will always be moves that that Pokémon is able to learn and the abilities are limited to those that that Pokémon can have.

Opponent

In each battle, the player will fight against an opposing Pokémon. Every turn, the opposing Pokémon will use a random move in its moveset.
